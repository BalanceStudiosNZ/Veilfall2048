About the Game

Step into the not too distant, dark future, where ancient magic lays strangled in the shadows and mud of a dystopic, military-industrial Slumtopia of the year 2048. This is only the beginning — join us on the journey as development continues.

Our Studio

Creatures of the Night is a 2 person team, hailing from New Zealand and Iran. We are interested in pushing available technology and redefining what Independent development can accomplish in the gaming sphere. We are currently developing a range of animation and mocap technology through Cascadeur, Move.ai and Unreal Engine 5 to remove hinderances for developers wanting comprehensive, realistic and grounded locomotion tools.
